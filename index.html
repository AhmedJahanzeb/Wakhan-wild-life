WELCOME

Welcome to Wakhan Wildlife, where we invite you to embark on an unforgettable journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Pakistan. Our wildlife tours offer an incredible opportunity to witness some of the world's rarest and most majestic creatures, including the elusive snow leopard. Not only will you have the chance to embark on a thrilling snow leopard expedition, but also witness other incredible wildlife sightings and even take part in trophy hunting under strict ethical guidelines. But that's not all - our commitment to conservation and biodiversity means that by choosing Wakhan Wildlife, you're not only having a once-in-a-lifetime experience, but also supporting our efforts to preserve these incredible species and their habitats. Join us in Gilgit Baltistan, where you'll discover a world of natural wonders and adventure. Our experienced guides will lead you on a journey through some of the most spectacular landscapes on earth, providing an immersive and educational experience like no other. So what are you waiting for? Come and explore with us, and discover the magic of Wakhan Wildlife.
